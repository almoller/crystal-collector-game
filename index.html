Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number. Each crystal contains a random hidden value. Click on each crystal to add it's value to your Number Sum.

Your goal is to collect the right combination of crystals so your number sum is equal to the given number.

If you can match the the given number exactly, you get a win.

If your number sum becomes greater than the given number, you get a loss.
